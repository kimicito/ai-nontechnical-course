Level 9 — Audit

9.5 Agent Scenarios for Audit

⏱ 12 min · agents for internal control and audit
How is an agent different from a language model?
LLM answers requests. Agent works continuously: monitors, compares, alerts, documents — without command "check now". More — Level 4.

Scenario 1: Anomaly Monitoring Agent (n8n + SQL)

Task: daily scan transactions, detect deviations from norm, send alerts to auditors.

[Cron: daily at 8:00]
↓
[SQL query to ERP: yesterday's transactions]
↓
[Agent: statistical analysis]
├─→ Amounts 2σ from average
├─→ Duplicate payments
├─→ Payments outside working hours
└─→ New counterparties with large amounts
↓
[LLM: formulate remark with context]
↓
[Email to auditor: "5 anomalies for 23.07"]
↓
[Google Sheets: anomaly log]
Result: anomalies detected within a day, not at quarterly reconciliation. Reaction time reduced from weeks to hours.

Scenario 2: Contract Audit Agent (OpenClaw Agent)

Task: upon contract submission for approval — automatically check for typical risks, template compliance, mandatory conditions.

[Email / ECM: new contract for approval]
↓
[Agent: text extraction (OCR if PDF)]
↓
[On-prem LLM: checklist verification]
├─→ Compliance with company template
├─→ Mandatory conditions presence (insurance, penalties, force majeure)
├─→ Risky wording (auto-renewal, sole arbitration)
├─→ Legislative compliance
└─→ Details check against business registry
↓
[Verification protocol formation]
├─ Remarks with item references
├─ Correction recommendations
└─ Risk assessment (low / medium / high)
↓
[Notification to auditor + lawyer]

Scenario 3: Audit Preparation Agent (Hybrid)

Task: before external audit collect all required documents, check completeness, prepare plan.

[Request: "Preparing for Q2 VAT audit"]
↓
[Agent: document search in ECM / archive]
├─→ Invoices
├─→ Purchase/sales ledger
├─→ Bank statements
└─→ Counterparty contracts
↓
[Completeness check by auditor's list]
↓
[LLM: analysis of found / missing documents]
↓
[Preparation plan formation]
├─ What's ready
├─ What's missing + responsible persons
└─ Deadlines
↓
[Notification to manager + responsible persons]
