8.3 Work and resource planning: schedules, people, equipment

🎯 No code · ✅ Interactive quiz

🎯 Why does business need this?

The work organizer (foreman, site manager) decides every day: who is working, on what, in what sequence, and what is delayed.AI helps build schedules, optimize resources, and predict delays.

Case: AI analyzed the 3-month schedule and warned: concrete work on the 5th floor will begin before the crane is released from the 4th.

📚 What we are studying

What AI does for a work organizer

  • 📅 Charts: Construction/adjustment of network diagram, critical path
  • 👷 Resources: Distribution of teams, equipment, materials among facilities
  • ⚠️ Risks: Warning about delays, overloads, resource conflicts
  • 📊 Reporting: Work log, work completion certificates, plan/actual comparison
  • 🔮 Forecast: When will the object end at the current pace?
  • 📋 Task order: Automatic generation based on graph

Example of a request to AI

Prompt for chart
"Analyze the construction schedule (file attached).

🛠️ How to apply

Scenario: Weekly Chart Review

Process
1. Upload to AI: schedule (MS Project/Excel), weekly work log, resources

Plan/actual comparison

AI compares daily:

  • Planned volumes vs completed
  • Start/end dates vs fact
  • Material consumption vs standards
  • Labor vs plan

If the deviation is > 10% - a warning with a recommendation.

👨‍💻 How to delegate to a developer

Task template

“We need an AI tool to manage construction schedules.

✅ Checklist for accepting the result

  • Is the critical path correct?
  • Are the risks real?
  • Is resource loading adequate?
  • Is the end date forecast realistic?
  • Are the work orders on schedule?
  • Is the format convenient for daily use?
