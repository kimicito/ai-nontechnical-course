AI for Non-Technical Specialists
Level 17 — HR

17.3 Hybrid Tasks

⏱ 15 min · standard/anonymized outside, personal inside
Hybrid Principle for HR
General structure, methodology, standard phrasing — outside. Specific names, salaries, evaluations, employee facts — inside.

Task 1: Reference Check — Structure Outside, Assessment with Name Inside

Hybrid: standard reference check structure and assessment criteria are universal. Final assessment of specific candidate by real recommender responses — on-prem.

Stage 1 — External AI (Structure)

Prompt:
Develop reference check structure for position [title] level [junior/middle/senior]. Include: recommender circle (manager, peers, clients), competency questions [list], assessment scale, results recording form. Format: ready HR template.

Stage 2 — On-Prem (Specific Candidate Assessment)

Prompt:
Reference check results for candidate [name] by structure above: [notes]. Assess by each competency. Form final conclusion with recommendation (hire/reject/additional interview).
Time saved: 30–40 minutes for structure preparation (done once per position type). Specific candidate assessment — 10 minutes instead of 30.

Task 2: Individual Development Plan — Template Outside, Employee Tie Inside

Hybrid: general IDP template, standard development tracks — universal. Tying to specific employee assessment results — on-prem.

Stage 1 — External AI (Template)

Prompt:
Develop IDP template for [level: specialist/manager] for [period]. Include: structure (goals, competencies, activities, timelines, criteria), standard development tracks (technical, managerial, soft skills). Format: fillable template.

Stage 2 — On-Prem (Personalization)

Prompt:
Fill IDP template for employee [name]. Data: position [title], assessment results [data], employee goals [goals]. Create specific plan with measurable criteria.

Task 3: Engagement Surveys — Questions Outside, Answer Analysis Inside

Hybrid: question phrasing is standard, can be public. Analysis of real employee responses — personal data + management sensitivity.

Stage 1 — External AI (Questions)

Prompt:
Develop employee engagement survey. 15 questions: 10 closed (scale 1–5), 5 open. Topics: job satisfaction, manager relationship, development, work/life balance, company recommendation as employer. Format: ready questionnaire.

Stage 2 — On-Prem (Analysis)

Prompt:
Analyze engagement survey results for [period]. Data: [aggregated results, no names for individual responses]. Highlight: overall index, problem areas by department, trends vs previous period, recommendations.
Important: analysis of open responses where employees may mention names or describe specific situations — on-prem only.

Task 4: Assessment Feedback — Principles Outside, Employee Facts Inside

Hybrid: general principles of constructive feedback formulation — universal. Content with real facts about specific employee — on-prem.

Stage 1 — External AI (Methodology)

Prompt:
Develop manager guide for conducting review meeting. Include: conversation structure, constructive feedback examples (SBI model: Situation-Behavior-Impact), phrasing examples for different scenarios (exceeds expectations, meets, growth area). Format: manager cheat sheet.

Stage 2 — On-Prem (Specific Meeting Preparation)

Prompt:
Prepare talking points for review meeting with employee [name]. Data: position [title], assessment results [data], period goals [goals]. Use SBI model. Formulate feedback by each competency.

Task 5: Offers and Employment Contracts — Template Outside, Real Conditions Inside

Hybrid: standard offer/contract template — universal. Real conditions (salary, name, date) — on-prem.

Stage 1 — External AI (Template)

Prompt:
Create standard offer template for position [title] in [industry]. Include: structure, mandatory sections (position, salary, probation, benefits), confidentiality wording, part-time conditions (if applicable). Format: template with markers [NAME], [SALARY].

Stage 2 — On-Prem (Filling)

Prompt:
Fill offer template for candidate [name]. Data: position [title], salary [amount], start date [date], probation period [period]. Check compliance with labor code.

Knowledge Check

Questions are standard (can be in cloud), answer analysis — on-prem
Because survey is conducted online
Because survey is anonymous

Question structure development
Specific candidate assessment by real responses
Assessment scale selection (1–5)

Because goals are personal

Because timelines differ for everyone
