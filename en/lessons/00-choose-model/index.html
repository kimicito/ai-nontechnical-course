0.2 · How to choose a model: ChatGPT, Claude, Kimi, Qwen

🎯 No code · ✅ Quiz at the end

🎯 Why does business need this?

Different models are like different tools.

After this lesson:
  • Choose a model for your task in 2 minutes
  • Understand why ChatGPT is not always the answer
  • Save $$$ without overpaying for a powerful model for simple tasks

📚 Comparison of models

🟢 ChatGPT (OpenAI)

✅ Station wagon.

⚠️ Sometimes he “smoothes out” answers, he is afraid of controversial topics.

Take it when: We need a universal assistant, correspondence, idea generation, simple analyses.

🟣 Claude (Anthropic)

✅ King of long documents.

⚠️ Doesn't know how to search the Internet in real time.

Take it when: Working with contracts, reports, audits - anything with a lot of text.

🔵 Kimi (Moonshot AI)

✅ Huge context (2 million tokens).

⚠️ Weaker in Russian.

Take it when: Working with Chinese suppliers/markets, analyzing huge amounts of data.

🟠 Qwen (Alibaba)

✅ Multilingual (25+ languages).

⚠️ More difficult to set up.

Take it when: Multilingual projects, on-premise hosting, integration with existing systems.

🛠️ Selection algorithm (1-2-3)

Step 1: What is the task?

  • Documents > 50 pages→ Claude or Kimi
  • Correspondence, ideas, simple analyzes → ChatGPT
  • Chinese market/suppliers→ Kimi or Qwen
  • Confidential data→ Qwen (on-premise) or GENAi

Step 2: What's your budget?

  • For free→ ChatGPT Free, Claude Free (limits)
  • $20/month→ ChatGPT Plus or Claude Pro
  • $50-200/month→ API + integrations (developer required)
  • On-premise→ Server + license (see Lesson 0.3)

Step 3: Test on your task

  • Take one real document/request from work
  • Run through 2-3 models (free versions)
  • Compare quality, speed, convenience
  • Choose a winner and subscribe

👨‍💻 How to assign a task to a developer

Sample: 
"You need to select an LLM for the task: [DESCRIPTION].

✅ Checklist for accepting the result

  • Does the model cope with real problems from your work?
  • Is the cost predictable and within budget?
  • Is there a fallback option (not tied to one supplier)?
  • Has the developer confirmed the possibility of integration?
  • Has the data migration process been tested if the vendor changes terms?

🧰 Selection tools

  • Chatbot Arena (lmarena.ai)— blind testing of models, vote for the best answers
  • Artificial Analysis— comparison of prices, speed, quality
  • Vellum— platform for comparing prompts between models
