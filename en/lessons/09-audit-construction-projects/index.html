🔍 LEVEL 9 · AUDIT, CONTROL AND COMPLIANCE

9.3 · Audit of construction and projects

🎯 No code · ✅ Interactive quiz

⚠️ Examples in this lesson are for educational purposes only. For uploading contracts, estimates, certificates, and other confidential documents, you must use GENAI (corporate data-protected solution), not open cloud services (ChatGPT, Claude, Kimi).

🎯 Why does business need this?

Construction projects are complex, multi-step processes with a lot of paperwork, deviations and risks.

AI audit of construction sites and projects— automatic comparison of fact with plan (estimate, schedule, project), detection of deviations >5% and early warning of red zones.

Example: AI checked 150 KS-2 acts for six months with estimates and identified 12 deviations: 7 - overconsumption of materials >10%, 3 - lack of appropriate work in the project, 2 - double payment for one act.

📚 What we are studying

Construction: what AI checks

  • Reconciliation of KS-2/KS-3 acts with estimates: Do the work performed comply with the estimate documentation?
  • Overconsumption of materials: actual consumption vs estimated consumption taking into account GESN/FER standards.
  • Compliance with the project: Is the work performed included in the project?
  • Deadlines and schedule: deviation from the planned schedule, delays on critical paths, risk of delivery failure
  • Double payments: the same act in different payments, duplication of positions
  • SNiP/GOST: compliance with standards (minimum thicknesses, grades of materials, tolerances)

Projects: what AI checks

  • Stages and deliverables: have all stages been completed, have all approvals been received, are all documents in place?
  • Budget: fact vs plan, deviation by item, forecast of final cost
  • Deadlines: deviation from baseline, trend (are we ahead or behind), risk of failure
  • Resources: team workload, overexpenditure of man-hours, unplanned resources
  • Red zones: automatic allocation: budget >110%, failure time >2 weeks, resources at 120%

🛠️ How to apply

Scenario: reconciliation of KS-2 acts with estimates

Prompt for AI
Check the KS-2 acts (file: acts.xlsx) with the estimate (file: estimate.xlsx).

Result: Excel file with verification of each act.

Scenario: monitoring projects in red zones

Prompt for AI
Analyze the status of projects (file: projects.xlsx).

Result: The project manager sees a dashboard with 20 projects: 12 green, 5 yellow, 3 red.

✅ Checklist for accepting the result

  • Are the acts verified with the estimate for volume and price?
  • Is excess consumption of materials considered taking into account GESN/FER standards?
  • Does the double payment check work (no false positives)?
  • Is the color rating clear without explanation?
  • Do red zones appear in alerts immediately?
  • Does the risk forecast show what will happen in a month?
  • Are all three sources (fact, plan, project) connected?
