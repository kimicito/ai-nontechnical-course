Level 15 — Legal Function

15.4 Practical Conclusion

⏱ 15 min · level summary
Main Principle of Level 15
Legal function — one of the most closed by default. Attorney-client privilege, insider information about deals and disputes require strict control. Environment decision should be made not only by CISO, but also by chief legal officer.

Key Takeaways

1. On-Prem — Default Rule

For lawyers, on-prem is not exception, but norm:

  • All documents on specific cases — claims, responses, complaints, legal positions
  • Due diligence of specific counterparties — especially M&A (insider information)
  • Corporate documents before publication — minutes, resolutions, reorganizations
  • Responses to regulators — company strategic position
  • Sanctions screening of specific deals — risk interpretation
  • Labor disputes — personal data, employer position

2. External AI — Only Open Legal Field

Safe tasks for cloud:

  • Standard contract templates (without real terms)
  • Legislation change monitoring (public sources)
  • Case law analysis (open decisions)
  • Patent search (open databases)
  • Training materials (general topics)
  • Legal practice benchmarking (public data)

3. Hybrid — For Complex Tasks

Three tasks work on hybrid scheme:

  • Legal opinion — legal logic outside, facts inside
  • Statements of claim — structure outside, specific case inside
  • Due diligence — public part outside, internal assessment inside

4. Unique Factor — Attorney-Client Privilege

Using external cloud service for case materials may raise questions about confidentiality breach and affect privilege defense in some jurisdictions. This is a separate (legal, not only IT security) argument for on-prem.

What Was Excluded from Level (and Why)

Automatic counterparty scoring from databases — rule-based/ML screening systems (OFAC, EU, national lists). This is result of database integration, generative AI connects only to interpretation/formatting of ready result.

Big data legal analytics — predictive case law analytics, ML risk assessment models. GenAI can interpret results, but doesn't replace the model.

Lawyer Checklist Before External AI

Before sending request to external AI, check:

  • ❌ No company-party names?
  • ❌ No specific amounts and prices?
  • ❌ No dates and terms of specific deals?
  • ❌ No employee or witness names?
  • ❌ No case numbers, registry numbers?
  • ❌ No strategic position on dispute?
  • ❌ No personal data?
  • ❌ No M&A insider information?

If any item fails — on-prem only.
