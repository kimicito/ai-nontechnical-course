2.1 · RAG in simple words: how AI searches your documents

🎯 No code · ✅ Interactive quiz

🎯 Why does business need this?

Standard ChatGPT doesn't know about your internal documents: contracts, regulations, price lists, protocols.

RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)- technology that allows AI to search for answers in your documents, and not on the general Internet.

Example: The employee asks, “What is the due date for supplier X?”

📚 What we are studying

How RAG works: 4 steps

1
Uploading documents

You upload PDF, DOCX, TXT to the system.

2
Indexing

AI breaks documents into pieces and creates a “map” - like a library catalog.

3
Search

When you ask a question, AI searches for relevant chunks in your documents.

4
Generating a response

AI composes an answer based on the fragments found + its knowledge.

Analogy for understanding

Without RAG: You are asking advice from someone who has read a lot of books but knows nothing about your company.

With RAG: You ask the secretary for advice, who has access to all company documents and instantly finds the information you need.

✅ Checklist for accepting the result

  • AI responds based on downloaded documents rather than general knowledge?
  • Does the answer indicate the source (document title, page)?
  • Does the search work by meaning, not just by exact words?
  • Are the results relevant (the first 3 answers are useful)?
  • Is there access control based on roles?
  • Does the system scale as documents grow?
