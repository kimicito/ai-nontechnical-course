Level 14 — Sales

14.5 Agent Scenarios for Sales

⏱ 12 min · scoring, proposals, forecasting
How is an agent different from a language model?
LLM writes text on request. Agent works cyclically: reads CRM, scores leads, forms proposals, sends — without manager involvement at each step. More — Level 4.

Scenario 1: Lead Scoring Agent (n8n + CRM)

Task: evaluate new CRM leads, prioritize, assign to managers.

[Trigger: new lead in CRM]
↓
[n8n: collect lead data]
├─→ Source (website, exhibition, referral)
├─→ Company data (industry, size)
├─→ Request (product, volume, deadline)
└─→ Interaction history
↓
[LLM: scoring]
├─→ Budget (1–5)
├─→ Authority (1–5)
├─→ Need (1–5)
├─→ Timeline (1–5)
└─→ BANT total score
↓
[Prioritization]
├─→ A (≥80 points): urgent → senior manager
├─→ B (50–79): within day → manager
└─→ C (<50): nurture → email campaign
↓
[Create task in CRM + notification]
Result: managers spend time on leads with >30% conversion probability, not all indiscriminately.

Scenario 2: Proposal Preparation Agent (OpenClaw Agent)

Task: upon client request automatically prepare personalized commercial proposal.

[Client request: email / website form / CRM]
↓
[Agent: extract requirements]
├─→ Product, volume
├─→ Specific requirements (certificates, packaging)
├─→ Deadlines
└─→ Client purchase history
↓
[On-prem LLM: form proposal]
├─→ Context-based salutation
├─→ Offer considering history
├─→ Terms (price, deadline, payment)
├─→ Benefits for this client
└─→ Call to action
↓
[PDF generation]
↓
[Email to client + task to manager: "Proposal sent, track response"]

Scenario 3: Demand Forecasting Agent (Hybrid)

Task: analyze sales history, market data, form forecast for planning.

[Cron: weekly]
↓
[Data collection]
├─→ Sales history (ERP)
├─→ Order pipeline (CRM)
├─→ Seasonality
└─→ Market data (external: indices, industry statistics)
↓
[On-prem agent: trend analysis]
├─→ Growth/decline by product
├─→ New trends
└─→ Anomalies (large one-time orders)
↓
[LLM: forecast formation]
├─→ Monthly
├─→ Quarterly
└─→ Risks and scenarios
↓
[Report: sales + production + procurement]
Important: sales history and order pipeline are trade secrets. Trend analysis — on-prem. Market data can be obtained externally.
