16.3 Hybrid Tasks

⏱ 20 min · for compliance officers
Hybrid Approach
General methodology, structure, standard logic is prepared in external AI.
Specific names, deals, transactions, strategic decisions — in on-prem.

Hybrid Approach Principle for Compliance

Compliance combines sensitivity from several functions. The hybrid approach allows using external AI for methodology and general logic while keeping confidential data inside.

Anonymization Rules for Cloud:

Account numbers, deal datesTypical industry timelines

Knowledge Check

Question 1: Why is sanctions screening hybrid, not fully in cloud?

Because methodology is public, but decision on specific name is confidential
Because screening requires specialized software
Can be fully in cloud if anonymized

Question 2: Compliance risk assessment — what goes in on-prem?

Application to real business processes and departments
Risk matrix development
Determining risk factor weights

Question 3: Training scenarios — why anonymize?

To avoid disclosing real incidents and involved persons
So the insurance company doesn't find out
So employees don't know it's a real case
