5.1 · ROI calculator: calculate the benefits of AI

🎯 No code · ✅ Interactive calculator

🎯 Why does business need this?

Management allocates budget when they see specific numbers.

ROI (Return on Investment)— return on investment ratio.

Formula:ROI = (Benefit - Cost) / Cost × 100%

Task template

“We need an ROI calculation for the project [name].

✅ Checklist for accepting the result

  • Are all costs taken into account?
  • Is the benefit measured in money and not in “efficiency”?
  • Is there a comparison of scenarios (optimistic/realistic/pessimistic)?
  • Payback< 12 месяцев?
  • Have the risks been taken into account and is there a mitigation plan?
  • Is the calculation clear without explanation?

🎯 The main thing from the lesson

ROI = language of guidance. Don’t say “AI will speed up work” - say “AI will save 960,000 rubles per year, it will pay for itself in 3.5 months.”
