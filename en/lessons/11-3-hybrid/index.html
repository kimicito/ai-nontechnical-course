Level 12 — Maintenance (Repairs and Maintenance)

12.3 Hybrid Maintenance Tasks

⏱ 20 min · for reliability engineers
Hybrid Approach
Overall structure and methodology are prepared in the external AI (Claude, ChatGPT).
Real data (equipment numbers, parameters, history) is added on-prem.

Principle of the Hybrid Approach for Maintenance

The key rule: the more unique and critical the equipment (electrolyzers, specific turbines) — the stricter the on-prem. For widely used equipment (pumps, conveyors), the methodological part can safely go to the external cloud.

Anonymization Rules for the Cloud:

1. Root-cause + FMEA (Hybrid)

Cloud: Ishikawa structure, cause categories, 5 Why template.
On-prem: filling with real parameters of a specific machine and operating history.

Step 1: Cloud — Template Preparation
Prepare a 5 Why template for investigating a failure in rotating equipment:

Level 1: What happened? [SPECIFY]
Level 2: Why did it happen? [SPECIFY]
Level 3: Why didn't the system prevent it? [SPECIFY]
Level 4: Why wasn't it detected earlier? [SPECIFY]
Level 5: Why does the process allow this? [SPECIFY]

Add an Ishikawa diagram (6M) with cause categories for each level.
Step 2: On-prem — Filling with Real Data
Fill in the 5 Why template for a specific case:

Equipment: Gearbox R-08 (Mill M-3 drive)
Failure: Bearing destruction (2026-07-10)

Data from inspection logs, repair history, MES parameters.
Conduct root-cause analysis, fill in Ishikawa, 5 Why.

Result: Completed RCA report for archive and management.

Time savings: Ready template in the cloud (2 hours) + Completed RCA on-prem (another 2 hours).
