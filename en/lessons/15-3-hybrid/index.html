Level 15 — Legal Function

15.3 Hybrid Tasks

⏱ 20 min · for lawyers
Hybrid Approach
General legal logic, document structure, standard argumentation are prepared in external AI.
Real facts, amounts, party names, strategic position are added in on-prem.

Hybrid Approach Principle for Lawyers

The legal function requires special caution: attorney-client privilege may be compromised when using external services. The hybrid approach allows leveraging the strengths of both environments without violating confidentiality.

Anonymization Rules for Cloud:
Must RemoveCan Keep
Company-party namesParty type (supplier, buyer, employer)
Specific amounts and pricesDamage calculation methodology
Dates and terms of specific dealsTypical timelines in industry
Employee and witness namesParticipant positions
Case numbers, registry numbersCase type (debt recovery, breach)
Strategic position on disputeGeneral legal logic

1. Legal Opinion (Hybrid)

Cloud: general legal logic, applicable norms, similar practice.
On-prem: real facts, amounts, specific business risks.

Step 1: Cloud — Legal Logic
Prepare legal analysis on typical situation:

Situation (anonymized):
Company in industry [SPECIFY TYPE] encountered issue:
[SPECIFY LEGAL ISSUE in general terms]

Jurisdiction: [SPECIFY]

Provide:
1. Applicable legal norms (articles, laws)
2. Case law on similar issues (anonymized)
3. Arguments "for" and "against"
4. Typical risks
5. General recommendations (without specific facts reference)

Format: legal analysis (2-3 pages)
Step 2: On-prem — Final Opinion
Fill legal opinion with real data:

Legal analysis (from cloud):
[PASTE CLOUD RESULT]

Real facts:
- Company: [SPECIFY NAME]
- Deal amount: [SPECIFY]
- Counterparty: [SPECIFY]
- Relationship history: [SPECIFY]
- Specific risks: [SPECIFY]

Adapt analysis:
1. Apply legal logic to real facts
2. Add specific figures (penalties, fines, terms)
3. Assess risk probability
4. Give specific recommendations with priorities
5. Action plan (who, what, when)

Time saved: analysis in cloud (2 hours) + final opinion in on-prem (1.5 hours).

Cloud: document structure, standard argumentation by case type.
On-prem: real facts, evidence, amounts, party names.

2. Typical plaintiff arguments
3. Typical defendant objections
4. Evidence usually required
5. Legal norms usually applied
6. Case law (typical court approaches)

[SPECIFY: industry, typical contract terms]
Step 2: On-prem — Specific Case
Adapt structure to specific case:

Case:
- Plaintiff: [SPECIFY]
- Defendant: [SPECIFY]
- Subject: [SPECIFY]
- Claim amount: [SPECIFY]
- Facts: [SPECIFY]
- Evidence: [SPECIFY]

Structure (from cloud):

Draft:

Time saved: structure in cloud (1.5 hours) + case in on-prem (2 hours).
