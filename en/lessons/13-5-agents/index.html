Level 13 — Logistics

13.5 Agent Scenarios for Logistics

⏱ 12 min · cargo tracking, routes, claims
How is an agent different from a language model?
LLM answers questions. Agent works continuously: tracks cargo, analyzes delays, warns — without command "where is my cargo?". More — Level 4.

Scenario 1: Cargo Tracking Agent (n8n + TMS API)

Task: track shipment statuses, warn of delays, update clients.

[Cron: every 30 minutes]
↓
[TMS / carrier API: active shipment statuses]
↓
[Agent: deviation analysis]
├─→ Cargo in transit longer than norm: warning
├─→ Status not updated >24h: query carrier
└─→ Cargo arrived: notify client
↓
[Automatic notifications]
├─ Client: "Cargo arrived at point X, expect delivery TOMORROW"
├─ Logistics: "Shipment N delayed 2 days, reason: [from API]"
└─ Warehouse: "Plan receiving for [date]"
↓
[Daily dashboard: all shipments, statuses, risks]
Result: clients learn about delays before they call. Save 20–30 calls per day.

Scenario 2: Route Optimization Agent (OpenClaw Agent)

Task: analyze historical route data, propose alternatives, evaluate cost.

[Request: "Optimize route City A → City B"]
↓
[Agent: data collection]
├─→ Historical routes (time, cost, incidents)
├─→ Current carrier rates
├─→ Border conditions (queues, weather)
└─→ Client requirements (deadline, temperature)
↓
[On-prem LLM: comparative analysis]
├─→ Option A: rail, time, cost, risks
├─→ Option B: road, time, cost, risks
└─→ Option C: multimodal, time, cost, risks
↓
[Recommendation with justification]
├─ Optimal by price
├─ Optimal by time
└─ Optimal by risk
↓
[Report to logistics]

Scenario 3: Carrier Claims Agent (Hybrid)

Task: collect delay and damage data, prepare claims, track response deadlines.

[Trigger: shipment status = "delivered"]
↓
[Agent: data collection]
├─→ Planned vs actual delivery date
├─→ Inspection acts (damages)
├─→ Tracking: actual route
└─→ Correspondence with carrier
↓
[On-prem LLM: claim basis assessment]
├─→ Delay > permissible: penalty calculation
├─→ Damage: assessment by act
└─→ Shortage: consignment note reconciliation
↓
[Automatic claim generation]
├─→ Contract references, clauses
├─→ Claim amount calculation
└─→ Response deadline
↓
[Send to carrier + create task in ECM]
↓
[Monitoring: no response by deadline → escalation]
Important: claims contain commercial information (amounts, contract terms). All processing — on-prem.
