✅ Checklist for accepting the result

  • Is the budget predictable and does not exceed 5% of IT costs?
  • Is there a 2× reserve for peak loads?
  • Have alternatives (cheaper models) been considered?
  • Do you understand the threshold when it is more profitable to switch to a local solution (> $500-1000/month)?
  • Is there a process for controlling expenses (who monitors, who blocks)?
