7.3 · Review of contracts and compliance with technical specifications: identify risks

🎯 No code · ✅ Interactive quiz

⚠️ Examples in this lesson are for educational purposes only. For uploading contracts, estimates, certificates, and other confidential documents, you must use GENAI (corporate data-protected solution), not open cloud services (ChatGPT, Claude, Kimi).

🎯 Why does business need this?

An error in a contract can cost millions: unspecified late fees, unclear acceptance conditions, lack of guarantees.AI checks contracts in minutes, finds risks, compares them with technical specifications - and warns about problems.

Case: There was no clause in the contract regarding penalties for late delivery.

AI verification checklist

  1. ✅ Details of the parties (TIN, KPP, OGRN)
  2. ✅ Subject of the agreement (coincides with the technical specifications)
  3. ✅ Price (with/without VAT, payment procedure)
  4. ✅ Delivery times (specific dates/periods)
  5. ✅ Late fees (percentage, maximum)
  6. ✅ Acceptance conditions (criteria, deadlines)
  7. ✅ Warranty period (from the date of delivery/commissioning)
  8. ✅ Force majeure (definition, order)
  9. ✅ Procedure for resolving disputes (arbitration, court)
  10. ✅ Compliance with technical specifications (all requirements are taken into account)
