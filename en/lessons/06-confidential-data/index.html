6.1 · Confidential data: what not to send to AI

🎯 No code · ✅ Interactive quiz

🎯 Why does business need this?

When you send a document to ChatGPT, it can become part of model training.

Real case: The employee sent an agreement with the counterparty, including commercial terms, to ChatGPT.

📚 What we are studying

What can't be sent to cloud AI

CategoryExamples
Personal dataPassports, TIN, addresses, telephone numbers
Trade secretPrices, margins, suppliers, clients
Banking informationDetails, payment orders
NDAsEverything is under NDA
Medical dataDiagnoses, medical histories

How to work safely with AI

Level 1: De-identification

Replace sensitive data before sending.

Example
Instead of: "Romashka LLC, INN 7701234567, price 500,000 ₽"

Level 2: Local Solution

Run AI on your servers.

  • Ollama - running models locally
  • Llama 3, Mistral - open-source models
  • Required: server with GPU

Level 3: Corporate Contracts

Enterprise versions with confidentiality guarantees.

  • ChatGPT Enterprise - no data used for training
  • Microsoft Copilot - within your Azure
  • Claude for Work - Corporate Agreements

🛠️ How to apply

Check algorithm before sending

  1. Does the text contain personal information?
  2. Are there trade secrets?
  3. Is there data under NDA?
  4. If yes to any question -> deidentify or use local solution

De-identification template

Before de-identification
Agreement No. 45 dated March 15, 2025
After de-identification
Agreement No. [N] dated [DATE]

👨‍💻 How to delegate to a developer

Task template

“We need a system for safe work with AI.

✅ Checklist for accepting the result

  • Do all employees know what not to send?
  • Is there a de-identification process?
  • Is your on-premises solution configured for sensitive data?
  • Does logging work?
  • Is there an automatic leak check?
  • Is the policy signed by all employees?

📝Checking understanding

Question 1: Is it possible to send a contract with prices to the free ChatGPT?

A) Yes, if you remove the company name
B) No, prices are a trade secret
C>Yes, ChatGPT is safe

Question 2: What is de-identification?

A>Replacement of confidential data with placeholders
B>Deleting a document
C>File encryption

Question 3: What level of protection is the most reliable?

A>Local solution (data does not leave the company)
B>Enterprise version of cloud AI
C>De-identification

🎯 The main thing from the lesson

Privacy is not an option, but an obligation. Do not send personal data, trade secrets and NDAs to cloud AI without protection.
