4.5 MCP: How AI connects to your systems

🎯 No code · ✅ Interactive quiz

🎯 Why does business need this?

You have CRM, 1C, Excel, email, instant messengers -dozens of systems.

MCP (Model Context Protocol) is a “bridge” between AI and your systems.

Without MCP: AI responds based on general knowledge.

With MCP: AI responds based on your real data from 1C, CRM, Excel.

🛠️ How to apply

Scenario: smart purchasing assistant

Task: The buyer asks: “Do I need to order packaging?”

With MCP: AI checks balances in 1C → Analyzes consumption → Looks at the delivery schedule → Gives a recommendation.

AI response
"Based on data from 1C:
< 500 units): 2 weeks
- Delivery time from current supplier: 3 weeks

⚠️ Recommendation: place order within 3 days.
Last year experience: when ordering after the critical date,
there were production downtimes for 2 days (losses 180,000 ₽)."

👨‍💻 How to delegate to a developer

Task template

“We need an MCP server to integrate AI with [system].

✅ Checklist for accepting the result

  • Does AI correctly read data from the system?
  • Do the responses contain actual data (not cache)?
  • No confidential data leakage?
  • Read-only access or write access too?
  • Is the response rate acceptable? < 5 seconds)
  • Is there request logging?
