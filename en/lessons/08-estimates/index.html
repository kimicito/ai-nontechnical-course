8.1 · Estimates and calculations: from local estimates to the final cost

🎯 No code · ✅ Interactive quiz

⚠️ Examples in this lesson are for educational purposes only. For uploading contracts, estimates, certificates, and other confidential documents, you must use GENAI (corporate data-protected solution), not open cloud services (ChatGPT, Claude, Kimi).

🎯 Why does business need this?

The estimator spends up to 60% of the time on routine: rewriting items from local estimates into consolidated ones, reconciling prices with GESN/FER, checking coefficients, recalculating when volumes change.

Case: An estimator for an object with 500 items spent 3 days on the summary.

📚 What we are studying

What AI does for an estimator

  • 📄 Extracts items from local estimates (PDF, Excel, scans)
  • 🔢 Compares prices with GESN / FER / regional collections
  • 📊 Combines many local estimates into one table
  • 🧮 Checks coefficients (amendments, indices, CP)
  • 💰 Recalculates the total when volumes or prices change
  • ⚠️ Finds duplicates, omissions, discrepancies

🛠️ How to apply

Scenario: summary of 10 local estimates

Process
1. We collect all local estimates into a folder (PDF, Excel, scans)

Calculation of the final cost

AI considers the complete chain:

  1. Direct costs (work + materials + mechanisms)
  2. Overhead costs (% of direct)
  3. Estimated profit (%)
  4. VAT (if applicable)
  5. Total taking into account all coefficients

👨‍💻 How to delegate to a developer

Task template

“We need an AI tool for the estimator.

✅ Checklist for accepting the result

  • Are all positions retrieved?
  • Have the prices been verified with GESN/FER?
  • Are the odds correct?
  • Are there any duplicates or omissions found?
  • Does the total amount match the manual calculation?
  • Is the format convenient for importing into an estimate program?
