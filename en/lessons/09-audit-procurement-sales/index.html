9.2 Audit of purchases and sales

🎯 No code · ✅ Interactive quiz

🎯 Why does business need this?

Purchasing and sales are points of maximum risk: overpayments, discounts without approval, fictitious suppliers, unreasonable compensation.

AI audit of purchases and sales— automatic analysis of all transactions for compliance with policies, search for anomalies and identification of risks.

Example: AI analyzed 200 purchases for the quarter and identified 8 transactions with risks: 3 - single sellers without justification, 2 - changes in supplier details, 3 - discounts above the limit.

📚 What we are studying

Procurement: what AI checks

  • Availability of 3 control points: are there three commercial proposals, do they comply with legal/policy requirements?
  • Limits: whether the purchase limit has been exceeded, whether the procedure has been chosen correctly (competition, auction, sole supplier)
  • Soleist's rationale: is there a memorandum, does it meet the criteria (designer supervision, technological uniqueness, urgency)
  • Related persons: checking suppliers for relatedness (founders, addresses, telephone numbers)
  • Supplier history: new counterparty, frequent changes of details, mass address

Sales: what AI checks

  • Reconciliation of shipment and payment: shipped - paid, are there any delays?
  • Discounts and compensation: do they exceed the limit, is there an agreement, who approved
  • Returns: abnormal frequency of customer returns, reasons (defect, change of mind, manager’s error)
  • Receivable: late payments, debt dynamics, clients with increasing debt
  • Price anomalies: sudden price changes, weekend discounts, atypical conditions

Risk signals: red, yellow, green

SignalProcurementSales
🔴 RedAssociated supplier, fake CP, change of detailsRefund >30%, bankrupt client, 50% discount without approval
🟡 YellowSole supplier without memorandum, new supplier, round sumDelay 15 days, frequent returns, price below cost
🟢 Green3 CP, verified supplier, everything according to the procedurePayment on time, typical discounts, stable client

🛠️ How to apply

Scenario: Monthly procurement audit

Prompt for AI
Analyze the monthly purchase register (file).

Result: Excel spreadsheet with the rating of each transaction.

Scenario: monitoring sales for anomalies

Prompt for AI
Analyze the sales register for the quarter.

Result: The sales manager receives a list of clients that require attention and deals that need to be renegotiated.

👨‍💻 How to delegate to a developer

Task template

“We need an AI audit of procurement and sales.

✅ Checklist for accepting the result

  • Are all trades rated (red/yellow/green)?
  • Is check 3 CP working correctly?
  • Are there related persons (not false positives)?
  • Are discounts compared to approved limits?
  • Are receivables updated in real time?
  • Do red signals come instantly (not after a week)?
  • Is the report understandable to the director (not just the auditor)?

🎯 The main thing from the lesson

AI transforms auditing from “check everything” to “check red.” Automatic rating of transactions, search for related parties, checking limits - this is not a replacement for the auditor, but his tool.
