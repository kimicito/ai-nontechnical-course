6.3 · AI policy of the company: rules of the game

🎯 No code · ✅ Interactive quiz

🎯 Why does business need this?

Without rules for using AI, a company risks:data leak, wrong decisions, violation of the law.

Example: Company without a policy: an employee sent client data to free ChatGPT → Leak → Fine of 500,000 rubles under Federal Law 152.

Company with a policy: employee knew not to → Used local solution → No leak.

📚 What we are studying

What should an AI policy contain?

ChapterContent
1. PurposeWhy do we use AI, what problems do we solve?
2. Allowed toolsWhat services can be used (ChatGPT Enterprise, local, etc.)
3. Prohibited dataWhat can't be sent to cloud AI
4. ProcessesIn what tasks can AI work autonomously and where a human is needed?
5. ResponsibilityWho is responsible for the result, who checks
6. TrainingHow often to train, who conducts

Example: data categories by access levels

🔴 Red (strictly local):

  • Clients' personal data
  • Trade secret
  • NDAs
  • Financial statements

🟡 Yellow (de-identification + Enterprise):

  • Internal regulations
  • Contract templates
  • Analytical reports

🟢 Green (can be in the cloud):

  • General knowledge, background information
  • Drafts without sensitive data
  • Educational materials

How to implement a policy

  1. Assemble the team: IT, lawyer, HR, business representatives
  2. Write a draft: Use the template above
  3. Agree with a lawyer: Compliance with 152-FZ, NDA
  4. Approve by management: Order / instruction
  5. Train your employees: Don't just “read”, but practice
  6. Check: Audit once a quarter

✅ Checklist for accepting the result

  • Has the policy been approved by management?
  • Have all employees read and signed?
  • Is there training (not just "read")?
  • Are technical restrictions set?
  • Is there a review and audit process?
  • Are violations recorded and processed?
