1.4 · Working with documents: analysis, summarization, comparison

🎯 No code · ✅ Interactive quiz

⚠️ Examples in this lesson are for educational purposes only. For uploading contracts, estimates, certificates, and other confidential documents, you must use GENAI (corporate data-protected solution), not open cloud services (ChatGPT, Claude, Kimi).

🎯 Why does business need this?

Manager reads on average 2-3 hours of documents per day: contracts, reports, commercial proposals, acts, protocols.

AI reduces this to 15-20 minutes:

  • Contract summarization: 30 min → 2 min
  • Comparison 2 CP: 1 hour → 5 min
  • Risk analysis: 2 hours → 10 min

Total savings: 3-4 hours per day

📚 What we are studying

3 types of document tasks

1. Summarization (compression)

Retell the essence of the document in several paragraphs.

💡 Prompt example
You are a lawyer.

Saving:30 min → 2 minutes

2. Comparison

Compare 2 or more documents based on specified criteria.

💡 Prompt example
You are a buyer.

Saving:1 hour → 5 minutes

3. Risk analysis

Find potential problems in the document.

💡 Prompt example
You are a corporate lawyer with 15 years of experience.

Saving:2 hours → 10 minutes

🛠️ How to apply

Scenario: Contract Analysis

Step 1: Upload the contract to AI (ChatGPT Plus, Claude Pro, or Kimi support files)

Step 2: Set the role and task

Full prompt
You are a corporate lawyer.

Step 3: Check the result (especially the risk assessment)

Step 4: Save the prompt template for future contracts

Scenario: CP comparison

Task: Select a supplier from 3 commercial offers.

Prompt
You are an experienced buyer.

👨‍💻 How to delegate to a developer

Task template

“We need a system for automatic analysis of contracts.

✅ Checklist for accepting the result

  • Have all the key points of the contract been taken into account?
  • Are the risks assessed correctly?
  • Are the recommendations applicable?
  • Is the format convenient for sharing with colleagues?
  • No hallucinations?
  • For critical contracts - checked by a lawyer?
