Level 14 — Sales & Marketing

14.4 Practical Conclusion

⏱ 15 min · level summary
Main Principle of Level 14
Sales and marketing are the mirror function of procurement. Symmetric data sensitivity (price, discount, terms = trade secret from either side of the deal). At the same time, marketing is one of the safest zones for external AI.

Key Takeaways

1. Sales = Procurement in the Mirror

Where in procurement you are the buyer with your negotiating position, in sales you are the seller with exactly the same data sensitivity structure:

  • Price and discounts — trade secret, regardless of which side of the deal
  • Payment terms — company financial policy
  • Negotiation strategy — BATNA, ZOPA, leverage not for external discussion
  • Client base — strategic asset, dependency on key clients

2. Marketing — Safe Zone for External AI

Marketing content is public by definition:

  • Posts, articles, brochures — public by nature
  • SEO optimization — working with public content
  • Competitor analysis from open data — legal and safe
  • Ad campaign copywriting — end product is public

Only restriction: don't send internal budgets, strategic plans, pricing policy to external AI before publication.

3. Demand Forecasting — More Sensitive Than It Seems

Sales forecasts directly flow into production planning, raw material procurement, inventory management. Forecast leak = operational plan leak. Always on-prem.

4. Hybrid — Foundation of Efficiency

The most common tasks (CP, negotiations, email campaigns, IR materials) work on hybrid scheme:

  • Template, structure, methodology — in external AI (fast, high quality)
  • Figures, clients, terms — in on-prem (safe)

What Was Excluded from Level (and Why)

Lead scoring and predictive churn analytics — supervised ML on CRM data. Generative AI can only interpret results, not replace the model.

Recommendation personalization — recommender systems (ML/collaborative filtering). GenAI connects to text generation based on ready recommendations.

Programmatic ad buying — algorithmic trading, real-time bidding. GenAI can help with creatives, but not with buying.

Checklist for Salesperson and Marketer

Before sending request to external AI, check:

  • ❌ No specific prices, discounts, payment terms?
  • ❌ No client names, contacts, phone numbers?
  • ❌ No volumes for specific deals?
  • ❌ No company financial indicators?
  • ❌ No sales forecasts, plans, budgets?
  • ❌ No win/loss analysis for specific deals?
  • ❌ No strategic plans before publication?

If any item fails — anonymize or send to on-prem.

Knowledge Check — Final Quiz

Question 1: Why is marketing safer than sales for external AI?

Because the end product (post, brochure) is intended for the public
Because marketers don't have access to prices
Because external models generate creatives better

Question 2: Which environment for campaign effectiveness analysis on internal metrics?

External AI — metrics are just numbers
On-prem — contains budgets, conversion, ROI
Hybrid — analysis methodology outside, metrics inside

Question 3: Can win/loss analysis be done in cloud if client is anonymized?

Yes, if client name and specific prices are removed
No, it reveals reasons for losing deals — critically sensitive
Only for won deals
