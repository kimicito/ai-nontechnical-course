Level 14 — Sales & Marketing

14.3 Hybrid Tasks

⏱ 20 min · for salespeople and marketers
Hybrid Approach
General structure and methodology are prepared in external AI.
Real data (prices, clients, terms) is added in on-prem.

Hybrid Approach Principle for Sales and Marketing

The key rule: the more sensitive the data (specific prices, deal terms, client personal data) — the stricter the on-prem. General methodology (document structure, typical scenarios) can safely go to the external environment.

Anonymization Rules for Cloud:
Must RemoveCan Keep
Specific prices and discountsPricing methodology
Client names and contactsClient type (large, medium, key)
Individual payment termsTypical payment forms
Volumes for specific dealsVolume calculation methodology
Company financial indicatorsIndustry benchmarks
Specific deal datesSeasonality, typical timelines
