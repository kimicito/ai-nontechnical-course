3.2 Integration of AI into business processes

🎯 No code · ✅ Interactive quiz

🎯 Why does business need this?

AI in isolation is a toy.AI in progress is a tool that works automatically without requiring intervention.

Example: Instead of copying data from an email into Excel, then analyzing it manually, then writing a report - AI does it all on its own while you drink your coffee.

📚 What we are studying

Where to implement AI in processes

ProcessWhat to automateSaving
Application processingClassification, routing, responses2-3 hours/day
Document flowChecking, comparing, archiving1-2 hours/day
ReportingData collection, analysis, formatting3-4 hours/week
CommunicationsDraft letters, translations, summaries1 hour/day

AI Adoption Maturity Model

Level 1 - Substitution: A person does the same, but with the help of AI (chat, prompts)

Level 2 - Acceleration: AI automates part of the task (summarization, tables)

Level 3 - Automation: AI works without human intervention (n8n, Zapier)

Level 4 - Rebuilding: Process revised to take advantage of AI capabilities

🛠️ How to apply

Algorithm for introducing AI into the process

  1. Select process: Repeats > 3 times/week, takes > 30 minutes
  2. Describe the current process: Who, what, when, how long
  3. Find the AI ​​application point: Where is the most handmade?
  4. Create a prototype: One prompt, one automation
  5. Measure the result: How much time did you save?
  6. Scale: Transfer to similar tasks

👨‍💻 How to delegate to a developer

Task template

“We need to introduce AI into the [name] process.

✅ Checklist for accepting the result

  • Has the process become faster?
  • Are there fewer errors?
  • Are the employees happy?
  • Is the result stable?
  • Is there documentation?
  • Can the process be scaled to other tasks?
