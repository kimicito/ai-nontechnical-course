🔍 LEVEL 9 · AUDIT, CONTROL AND COMPLIANCE

9.4 · Compliance, fraud monitoring and audit reports

🎯 No code · ✅ Interactive quiz

⚠️ Examples in this lesson are for educational purposes only. For uploading contracts, estimates, certificates, and other confidential documents, you must use GENAI (corporate data-protected solution), not open cloud services (ChatGPT, Claude, Kimi).

🎯 Why does business need this?

Regulations are constantly changing, and inspections come unexpectedly.

AI for compliance and fraud monitoring— automatic tracking of changes in laws, monitoring transactions for anomalies, preparing an evidence base and generating audit reports in hours, not weeks.

Example: AI checks 10,000 transactions daily and has identified 23 suspicious ones in a month: 7 - exit payments, 12 - change of counterparty details, 4 - circular schemes (you pay A → A pays B → B pays you).

📚 What we are studying

Tracking changes in regulations

  • Monitoring laws: AI monitors changes in tax, labor, construction legislation, GOST, SNiP, 44-FZ/223-FZ
  • Checking internal policies: Do internal regulations comply with the new requirements?
  • Alerts: notification to those responsible when a change requires action (new report, new threshold, new procedure)

Fraud monitoring: risk signals

  • Weekends and holidays: Payments on Saturday/Sunday are rare, but it does happen.
  • Change of details: the counterparty suddenly changed the bank, TIN, address - requires verification.
  • Circular patterns: A pays B, B pays C, C pays A - AI builds a graph of connections and finds cycles.
  • Suspicious counterparties: new (registered< 3 месяцев), массовые адреса, связанные учредители, статус «в процессе ликвидации».
  • Amount anomalies: round (500,000, 1,000,000), fractional with kopecks (123,456.78), a sharp deviation from the average.

Preparation for inspections and audit reports

  • Collection of evidence base: at the request of the regulatory authority, AI collects: a register of contracts, acts, payments, correspondence - in one package
  • Structuring answers: generating a response to a request using a template, with numbering, links to documents
  • Generating an audit report: AI collects a summary report from expert opinions with risk ranking (high/medium/low) and recommendations
  • Presentation to the Board of Directors: key figures, trends, 3 main risks, recommendations - on 5 slides

🛠️ How to apply

Scenario: fraud transaction monitoring

Prompt for AI
Analyze the bank statement (file) for the month.

Result: The head of financial monitoring receives a daily report with 23 suspicious transactions out of 10,000. The response time is hours, not weeks.

Scenario: preparing for a tax audit

Prompt for AI
A request came from the tax office: “Provide documents on the counterparty LLC Alfa for 2025.”

Result: Instead of 2 weeks of searching - 2 hours.

✅ Checklist for accepting the result

  • Are all 6 types of fraud signals checked?
  • Are there any circular patterns (not false positives)?
  • Do alerts come instantly (not every other day)?
  • Does regulatory tracking cover all the necessary laws?
  • Collecting documents for verification costs< 2 часов?
  • Is the registry clear without explanation (by itself)?
  • Does the audit report rank risks (high/medium/low)?
  • Presentation for the board of directors - 5 slides, not 50?

🎯 The main thing from the lesson

AI does not prevent fraud, but it detects it in hours, not months. Regulatory tracking, transaction monitoring, collection of documents for inspections, generation of audit reports - all this is a routine that AI does systematically and without fatigue.
