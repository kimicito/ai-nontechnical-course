Generative AI capabilities for B2B are constantly evolving. Today, you shouldn't ignore this technology in your work, which is why I decided to create this training course.

It includes knowledge from training courses by Anthropic (Claude), OpenAI (ChatGPT), and Google (Gemini), as well as my experience training my colleagues to work with Generative AI.

I understand that not everyone has programming or systems analysis skills, but there is another equally valuable expertise — domain-specific, specialized — and it has become even more valuable with the advent of AI. Lawyers, procurement specialists, auditors, finance professionals, and colleagues from other specialties — this course is for you. Together with my AI agent named Kimicito, we have compiled it and are constantly improving it based on feedback. You can write to me directly at @tagartur and together we will create a useful corporate course on "AI for Non-IT Professionals."

AI Without Programming

Learn to use artificial intelligence in your work: automate routine tasks, delegate clear assignments to developers, and make data-driven decisions. No code. No math. Just practice.
