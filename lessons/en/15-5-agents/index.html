Level 15 — Legal

15.5 Agent Scenarios for Legal

⏱ 12 min · legislation monitoring, contract checking, due diligence
How is an agent different from a language model?
LLM analyzes a document on request. Agent works continuously: monitors laws, checks new contracts, alerts on risks — without command "check this". More — Level 4.

Scenario 1: Legislation Monitoring Agent (n8n + RSS)

Task: track law changes, court practice, regulatory acts; form digest for lawyers.

[Cron: daily at 8:00]
↓
[Parallel source polling]
├─→ RSS government portal (new orders)
├─→ Constitutional court (new decisions)
├─→ Supreme court (practice reviews)
├─→ Legal databases (bills)
└─→ International sanctions lists
↓
[LLM: filter by company profile]
├─→ Industry laws
├─→ Corporate law
├─→ Labor legislation
└─→ Sanctions (if applicable)
↓
[Impact assessment]
├─→ Critical: requires immediate action
├─→ Important: requires analysis
└─→ Informational: for reference
↓
[Digest: "Legislative changes for 22.07"]
↓
[Email to lawyers + publish in corporate knowledge base]
Result: lawyers learn about changes within a day, not a month. Company reacts first to regulatory risks.

Scenario 2: Contract Checking Agent (OpenClaw Agent)

Task: upon contract submission for approval — automatically check for risks, template compliance, prepare protocol.

[ECM: new contract for review]
↓
[Agent: text extraction]
├─→ OCR if scan
└─→ Parsing if Word/PDF
↓
[On-prem LLM: verification]
├─→ Compliance with company template
├─→ Mandatory conditions presence
├─→ Risky wording
├─→ Legislative compliance
└─→ Counterparty check (database integration)
↓
[Verification protocol]
├─→ Remarks with clause references
├─→ Risk level
├─→ Recommendations
└─→ Comparison with previous versions
↓
[Notification to lawyer + note in ECM]

Scenario 3: Due Diligence Agent (Hybrid)

Task: upon counterparty check request — collect public data, form profile, assess risks.

[Request: "Check Company X, tax ID 7701234567"]
↓
[Agent: public data collection]
├─→ Business registry: status, founders, director
├─→ Credit bureau: finances, litigations
├─→ Company website: activity, clients
├─→ News: negative, M&A
└─→ Sanctions lists: check
↓
[On-prem LLM: risk analysis]
├─→ Financial stability
├─→ Reputational risks
├─→ Sanctions risks
└─→ Recommendation (approve / restrictions / reject)
↓
[PDF counterparty profile]
↓
[Notification to procurement / lawyer]
Important: public data is collected externally, but profile and risk assessment are formed internally. The very fact of checking is internal information.
