🎯 Why does business need this?

AI can provide answers in any format.

Problem: The manager receives the analysis in text form and spends 15 minutes transferring it to the table.

Solution: Ask the AI ​​to immediately produce a table. 15 minutes × 20 tasks = 5 hours per week.

📚 What we are studying

4 output formats

📊 Table

Comparison, analysis, reports.

📋 JSON

For programmers and automation.

☑️ Checklist

For inspection, audit, quality control.

📝 Markdown

For documentation, instructions, articles.

How to ask for a checklist

Task template

✅ Checklist for accepting the result

  • Does the format match the request?
  • Are all fields filled in?
  • Is the data correct?
  • Can I copy it to Excel / 1C / CRM?
  • Is the structure clear without explanation?
  • Are the units of measurement specified?
